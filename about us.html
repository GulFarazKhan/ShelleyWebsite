About Us
Shelley'sfood is Pakistan’s first online catering service which follows a proper online business model. Shelley'sfood is an initiative by DeenFoods and Catering, a well-known name in the industry of catering and culinary across twin cities of Rawalpindi , Islamabad and Karachi . With an experience of almost five decades Deen Foods is not new to the business but has initiated the convenience of on click ordering.


We at Daig strive for extraordinary food quality with exceptional taste. Our focus is on providing convenience to our customers with a time saving approach. Affordability of one of the key factors which makes our customers choose us.
